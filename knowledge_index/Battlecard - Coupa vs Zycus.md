# Coupa vs. Zycus Competitive Battlecard

- **doc_type:** playbook
- **one_liner:** An internal competitive battlecard providing company comparisons, Coupa's strengths and weaknesses, objection-handling scripts, and 12 named win stories for use when Zycus is competing directly against Coupa.
- **use_when:** Active deal where Coupa is on the shortlist or has been selected; prospect mentions Coupa's market share, analyst position, or implementation speed; prospect raises Coupa's supplier network or integration breadth; rep needs ready responses to Coupa's common objections; deal involves supplier relationship management concerns.
- **covers:**
  - Company comparison table: Zycus (private, profitable, bootstrapped since 2001, 1,800+ employees, 300+ enterprise customers, Gartner Visionary 2025) vs. Coupa (PE-owned by Thoma Bravo $8B 2023, 3,400+ employees, 3,500+ customers all sizes, Gartner Leader 2025)
  - Coupa strengths and Zycus counter-strategies (6 areas): market leadership/analyst recognition, implementation speed, integration breadth, user adoption, analyst position, training/certification
  - Coupa weaknesses and Zycus attack strategies (6 areas): supplier experience, TCO/hidden costs, UX, platform flexibility, customer support (chat-only), implementation quality
  - Objection-handling scripts (8 objections with scripted responses)
  - 12 named win stories vs. Coupa (see citable facts)
