# GEP vs. Zycus Worldwide Competitive Battlecard

- **doc_type:** playbook
- **one_liner:** An internal competitive battlecard with company comparisons, GEP's strengths and weaknesses, objection-handling scripts, and 10 named win stories for deals where Zycus competes against GEP Worldwide.
- **use_when:** Active deal where GEP is on the shortlist; prospect values end-to-end transformation including managed services or consulting; prospect mentions GEP's Fortune 500 track record or Gartner Leader status; prospect is in pharma, oil & gas, or manufacturing (GEP's strong verticals); deal involves mid-market buyer where GEP's $500K+ pricing is a concern.
- **covers:**

  - GEP weaknesses and Zycus attack strategies (6 areas): slow deployment, poor UX, premium pricing, over-customization, limited transparency, Microsoft-centric architecture
  - Objection-handling scripts (6 objections)
  - 10 named win stories vs. GEP
  - Key takeaways: what to emphasize vs. GEP, what to avoid competing on, best opportunity profiles
