# SAP Ariba vs. Zycus Competitive Battlecard

- **doc_type:** playbook
- **one_liner:** An internal competitive battlecard with company comparisons, Ariba's strengths and weaknesses, objection-handling scripts, and 12 named win stories for deals where Zycus competes against SAP Ariba.
- **use_when:** Active deal where SAP Ariba is on the shortlist or currently in use; SAP-heavy prospect asks "why not just use Ariba?"; prospect raises Ariba's 5.3M supplier network, Fortune 500 credibility, or SAP ecosystem lock-in; deal involves non-SAP ERPs where Ariba's middleware complexity is a pain point; mid-market prospect finds Ariba over-engineered or too expensive.
- **covers:**
  - Company comparison: SAP Ariba (29.4% global market share, part of SAP SE €17.1B revenue 2024, $205/user/month average, 6–18 month implementations, Joule AI 2024, IDC Leader) vs. Zycus (2.4% market share, private/profitable since 2014, Princeton NJ/Pune India, 3–12 month implementations, 6+ years Merlin AI, Forrester Leader SVM Q3 2024, 89% G2 satisfaction vs. Ariba 83%)
  - Ariba strengths and Zycus counter-strategies (6 areas): supplier network, SAP integration, enterprise security, scale, module coverage, analyst recognition
  - Ariba weaknesses and Zycus attack strategies (8 areas): poor UX/adoption, slow implementation, non-SAP integration, TCO, limited GenAI, slow support, limited analytics, mid-market fit
  - Objection-handling scripts (6 objections)
  - 12 named win stories vs. Ariba (several also vs. other competitors)
