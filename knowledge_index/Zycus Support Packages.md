# Zycus Support Packages

- **doc_type:** showpad_asset
- **one_liner:** A concise overview of Zycus's three post-sale support tiers (Professional, Enterprise, Premium), detailing support hours, coverage model, incident management scope, and value-realization services included in each package.
- **use_when:** Prospect or customer asks what support is included in the SaaS contract; late-stage deal where procurement/IT is evaluating support SLAs; prospect has had a bad support experience with a competitor (e.g., Coupa's chat-only support, SAP Ariba's 2–4 month resolution times); renewal conversation involving support upsell from Professional to Enterprise or Premium.
- **covers:**
  - Three support tiers: Professional (included in SaaS fees), Enterprise (additional cost), Premium (additional cost)
  - Support hours for Severity 1: all tiers 24×7
  - Support hours for other severities: Professional 9×5, Enterprise 12×5, Premium 24×5
  - Support channels: email (all tiers), phone (all tiers), chat (all tiers), online portal (all tiers)
  - Support model: Professional = shared services; Enterprise = designated Procurement Analyst; Premium = dedicated Procurement Analyst + designated TAM
  - Active Incident Management scope by severity level per tier
  - Escalation Management and Operational Reviews coverage per tier
  - Success Management services (Enterprise + Premium): Go-Value Metrics, Comprehensive Suite Adoption Review, Adoption Booster Trainings, Release Assistance
  - Comprehensive Value Realization services (Premium only): Procurement Maturity assessment, Value Workshop, Partnership Program, CXO Leadership Connect
  - Frequency details: Go-Value Metrics quarterly (Enterprise) / monthly (Premium); Release Assistance proactive 1x/year (Enterprise) / 2x/year (Premium); Value Workshop once per renewal (Enterprise) / once a year (Premium); CXO Leadership Connect 2x/year (Premium); Partnership Program once a year (both)

- **citable_facts:**
  - Professional support is included in SaaS fees; Enterprise and Premium incur additional cost (slide 2)
  - All tiers provide 24×7 support for Severity 1 (Critical) incidents (slide 2)
  - Professional support is delivered in a shared model to Customer admins, in English (slide 2)
  - Enterprise tier includes a designated Procurement Analyst; Premium tier includes a dedicated Procurement Analyst plus a designated TAM (slide 3)
  - Premium support covers active incident management for Severity 1, 2, and 3; Enterprise covers 1 and 2; Professional covers Severity 1 only (slide 3)
  - Enterprise and Premium include Go-Value Metrics reviews (quarterly for Enterprise, monthly for Premium) and Adoption Booster Trainings (slide 4)
  - Premium includes CXO Leadership Connect (2x/year) and a Procurement Maturity assessment (slide 6) **(verify these are still current offer terms)**
