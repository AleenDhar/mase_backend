# Zycus TAM-CAM Customer Support Model

- **doc_type:** showpad_asset
- **one_liner:** A customer-facing deck explaining Zycus's post-sale success model, covering the roles of the Customer Account Manager (CAM) and Technical Account Manager (TAM), the full value-driven customer success lifecycle, and differentiated offerings including aVOC and AppXtend.
- **use_when:** Prospect asks what ongoing support and customer success look like post go-live; evaluating Zycus's commitment to ROI and value realization after implementation; procurement leader or CPO persona wants to understand escalation paths and executive engagement; competitor (e.g., Coupa) is being criticized for poor support; renewal or expansion conversation.
- **covers:**
  - Customer Engagement Model: dual-track CAM (relationship/adoption/ROI) and TAM (technical/support delivery) structure
  - CAM responsibilities: trusted advisor, customer advocate, product enhancement collaboration, project lifecycle roadmap, escalation point, Quarterly Business Reviews, Steering Committee engagement; touch points across Delivery/Operations, Product Management, Marketing, Tech Support, Finance
  - CAM adoption/ROI activities: Success Plan Alignment, contract management, change requests
  - TAM responsibilities: support delivery management, regular solution roadmaps, Monthly Business Reviews, stakeholder engagement (half-yearly), best practice sharing
  - TAM adoption/ROI activities: monthly usage metrics, KPI alignment, service delivery plan, ROI quantification
  - Full customer lifecycle stages: Design → Onboarding → Go-Live → Value Sustenance → Value Realization → Value Expansion
  - Services mapped to lifecycle: Proactive Support, Premium Support, Site Reliability Engineering, Release Management, Value Engineering, Adoption & Advisory Consulting, Voice of Customer (aVOC), Custom Extensions / AppXtend, Health Score Card, Loyalty (Praemia Program), Customer Advocacy & Peer Networking, Product Footprint Expansion, Renewal Management
  - Customer Success Framework (5 pillars): Technical Support (Self-Help, Onboarding, KEDB FTR, Realtime Collaboration), Customer Advocacy (CPO AI Council, CxO, Community), TAM (Hypercare, Consulting, Data Insights, Business Process Review), Value Engineering (Maturity Models, Benchmarking, Value Delivery), Adoption & Advisory Consulting
  - aVOC (Accelerated Voice of Customer): 4-step process (Initiate → Nominate → Vote → Publish); community-led product development; features prioritized by usage patterns and market trends
  - AppXtend: DIY low-code composable platform; app store experience; enables custom procurement apps; mix-and-match pre-built apps; external developer/partner buildout
