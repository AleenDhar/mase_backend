# iSwitch: Zycus Change Management Approach

- **doc_type:** showpad_asset
- **one_liner:** A structured overview of Zycus's iSwitch change management methodology, covering two service models (iSwitch Communication and iSwitch Local), the five-phase framework, RACI, deliverables, and specific training scope metrics.
- **use_when:** Prospect's procurement leader or HR/change team asks how Zycus manages user adoption and organizational change during and after implementation; deal involves a large-scale rollout with many end users or suppliers; prospect has failed a previous procurement implementation due to poor adoption; competitor is being positioned as having a faster/easier rollout; late-stage deal where implementation methodology is under scrutiny.
- **covers:**
  - iSwitch five-phase framework: Change Management Strategy → Communication → Training → Rollout → Feedback Loop
  - Two service models:
    - **iSwitch Communication** (lighter): covers Plan (6 communication deliverables), Action (2 items), Sustain (3 items)
    - **iSwitch Local** (full change management for one rollout): covers Case for Change, Plan (full strategy + stakeholder meetings), Action (rollout + tracking), Sustain (handover to client)
  - Deliverables: Communiqués & Newsletters, Stakeholder Management, Product USPs, Role-Wise USPs, Supplier Communication, Custom Manuals, Byte-Sized Videos
  - Strategy phase: RACI finalized in first meeting; customer assigns Single Point of Contact (SPC); Zycus gathers requirements per sprint
  - Communication phase: all deliverables in English; supplier communication in scope; triggered by customer SPC via common alias
  - Training phase: Train-the-Trainer approach; Zycus University platform; Zycus evaluates and certifies champions; sessions recorded and shared
  - Training scope metrics: Pilot = 20 champions (1 batch) + 100 suppliers (1 session, 1 hour each); Per rollout = 20 champions (1 batch) + 400 suppliers (4 sessions, 1 hour each)
  - Rollout phase: roll-out strategy, user assistance, training execution
  - Feedback loop: user adoption KPIs, training effectiveness assessment, post-go-live user onboarding support
  - Zycus University referenced as the e-learning platform

- **citable_facts:**
  - Zycus's iSwitch change management service covers strategy, communication, training, rollout, and ongoing feedback loop (slide 2)
  - Two iSwitch models: iSwitch Communication (communication-focused, lighter scope) and iSwitch Local (full change management for one rollout) (slide 3)
  - iSwitch training uses a Train-the-Trainer approach through Zycus University (slide 2)
  - Training deliverables include: custom manuals, byte-sized videos, e-learning content via Zycus University; sessions are recorded and shared (slide 2, 7)
  - Per-rollout training capacity: 20 champions trained in 1 batch; 400 suppliers trained across 4 one-hour sessions (slide 7)
  - Supplier communication is explicitly in scope (slide 6)
  - All deliverables are in English (note: scope does not include multilingual content without separate agreement) (slide 6)
